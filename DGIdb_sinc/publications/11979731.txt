today, bronchial asthma is considered as a chronic inflammatory disease of the airway. it has been revealed that various chemical mediators are involved in the onset of bronchial asthma. among them, particularly, the peptide leukotrienes, ltc４, ltd４ and lte４, have been known to play a pathophysiological

parenchyma and isolated human bronchi.  xxxd3545xxx  also demonstrated dose-dependent inhibition of
ltd４-induced dyspnea in guinea pigs in vivo.  xxxd3545xxx  demonstrated preventive and alleviating effects
on ltd４- and ovalbumin-induced decrease in the lung function.  xxxd3545xxx  also displayed inhibitory effects on ltd４-induced eosinophil infiltration into airway tissues and bronchial edema. in sheep naturally

asthma patients. as above,  xxxd3545xxx  is effective for treatment of bronchial asthma, which is attributed to
