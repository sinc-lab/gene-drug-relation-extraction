effect of cimetidine on basal and  xxxd324xxx -stimulated gastric acid secretion in peptic ulcer.  the effect of cimetidine, a histamine h2-receptor antagonist, on basal and beta-zole-stimulated gastric acid secretion was studied in 28 patients with peptic ulcer. the results demonstrate that cimetidine (300 mg im), is a potent inhibitor of both basal and  xxxd324xxx -stimulated gastric acid secretion.
